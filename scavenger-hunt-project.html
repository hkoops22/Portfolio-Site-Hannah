Scavenger Hunt App for CareerFoundry Course

To expand my design and marketing skills, I took CareerFoundryâ€™s UX Immersion course. One achievement of the course was to design an app for a scavenger hunt game. The process began as all do, with research. I studied current scavenger hunt apps on the market, designed user personas, and conducted surveys and interviews. With the foundation established, I built user flows and structured the information architecture of the app. I informed the app navigation with a card sort exercise. From there, I built wireframes which turned into prototypes running tests and producing new iterations along the way. You can see the most recent iteration of the app here: https://bit.ly/39rum7v

Project Timeline

  • Weeks 1-2: Research users and similar apps
  • Weeks 2-4: Design user flows and information architecture for the app
  • Week 5-6: Solidify app navigation
  • Week 7-9: Build wireframes, Build initial prototype and test
  • Weeks 9-12: Gather feedback and adapt prototype into new iterations
  • Weeks 13-15: Build final app iterations and create Behance portfolio for project
